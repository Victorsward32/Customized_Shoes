About Us

Welcome to Saloni Footwear!

At Saloni Footwear, we specialize in crafting uniquely designed and meticulously handcrafted footwear to cater to diverse needs and occasions. Located at 03, Anant Park Building, opposite Metro Hospital, Diva Agasan Road, Diva (E), we pride ourselves on delivering superior quality shoes that blend elegance, comfort, and durability.

Our Offerings:

  • Specially Customized Leather Shoes: Tailored to perfection, our leather shoes are designed to provide the ultimate in comfort and style, reflecting your personal taste and needs.
  • Doctor Shoes: Prioritizing comfort and support, our doctor shoes are crafted to meet the specific requirements of medical professionals who are on their feet for extended periods.
  • Kolhapuri: Embracing traditional craftsmanship, our Kolhapuri shoes are a perfect blend of heritage and contemporary fashion, making them a versatile addition to your wardrobe.
  • Wedding Mojdi: Our exquisite wedding Mojdi collection ensures that you step into your special day with grace and elegance, featuring intricate designs and premium materials.

Our Mission:

At Saloni Footwear, our mission is to create high-quality footwear that not only looks great but also feels amazing with every step you take. We are dedicated to combining fashion and function to ensure our customers experience the best in footwear.

For inquiries or to place an order, please contact us at +91 91365 57937. We look forward to providing you with the perfect pair of shoes that meet your unique requirements.
